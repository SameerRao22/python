LIST_NAME.APPEND('ITEM') PUTS THE ITEM AT THE END OF THE LIST

LIST_NAME.POP(INDEX) DELETES ITEM AND RETURNS IT

LIST_NAME.REMOVE('ITEM') REMOVES ITEM

LIST_NAME.INSERT(INDEX 'ITEM') INSERTS ITEM AT THE INDEX SPECIFIED

LEN(LIST_NAME) GIVES LENGTH OF THE LIST
